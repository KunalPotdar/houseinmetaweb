About the Role

We're looking for a talented 3D Creative Artist to join our innovative team. You'll work on real-world real estate projects, creating immersive 3D experiences that help clients visualize properties before they're built.

This role is perfect for creative minds who love pushing the boundaries of visualization technology and want to make a real impact in the real estate industry.

Key Responsibilities

3D Building Modeller

Create detailed, lifelike architectural models from blueprints and reference materials. Ensure accuracy and attention to detail in every structure.

3D Creative Designer

Transform conceptual ideas into stunning immersive visuals. Design interior and exterior spaces that captivate and inspire potential buyers.

Rendering Artist

Bring realism to 3D scenes through expert lighting, texturing, and material application. Produce photorealistic renders that showcase properties in their best light.

Walkthrough Expert

Create interactive virtual experiences and cinematic walkthroughs. Enable clients to explore properties remotely with smooth, engaging navigation.

Requirements

Technical Skills:
  • Proficiency in 3D software (Blender, 3ds Max, Maya, or Cinema 4D)
  • Experience with rendering engines (V-Ray, Corona, Cycles, or similar)
  • Strong understanding of lighting, texturing, and materials
  • Knowledge of architectural visualization best practices
Portfolio:
  • Demonstrated experience in 3D architectural visualization
  • Portfolio showcasing photorealistic renders and/or walkthroughs
  • Examples of completed real estate or architectural projects preferred
Soft Skills:
  • Strong attention to detail and eye for aesthetics
  • Ability to work independently and meet deadlines
  • Good communication skills for remote collaboration
  • Creative problem-solving mindset
Bonus Points:
  • Experience with real-time 3D engines (Unity, Unreal Engine)
  • Web-based 3D visualization knowledge (Three.js, WebGL)
  • VR/AR experience in real estate applications
  • Motion graphics and animation skills
