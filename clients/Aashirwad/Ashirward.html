Project Overview

Ashirward represents a milestone in modern real estate marketing, featuring our cutting-edge 3D property visualization and interactive customization platform. This high-end residential property showcases the power of immersive digital experiences in accelerating pre-sales and enhancing buyer engagement.

Key Features

  • Interactive 3D exterior and interior visualization
  • Real-time customization of materials, colors, and finishes
  • Virtual walkthrough experience
  • Web-based platform accessible from any device
  • High-quality photorealistic rendering
  • Seamless user interface for property exploration

Results

Our 3D visualization solution enabled potential buyers to explore and customize the property from anywhere in the world, significantly reducing the need for physical site visits while increasing buyer confidence and accelerating the sales process.
