8. Your Data Rights (GDPR Articles 15-21)

Under GDPR, you have the following rights. To exercise any of these, contact us at privacy@houseinmeta.com:

1. Right to Access (Article 15)

What it means: You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

How to request: Email privacy@houseinmeta.com with "Data Access Request" in the subject.

Response time: We will provide your data within 30 days.

2. Right to Rectification (Article 16)

What it means: You can correct or update inaccurate personal data.

How to request: Contact us with corrections needed.

Response time: Changes made immediately.

3. Right to Erasure (Article 17)

What it means: You can request deletion of your personal data (Right to be Forgotten).

Exceptions: Data may be retained if required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

How to request: Email privacy@houseinmeta.com with "Deletion Request" in the subject.

Response time: We will delete your data within 30 days.

4. Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

What it means: You can receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON/CSV).

How to request: Email privacy@houseinmeta.com with "Data Export Request" in the subject.

Response time: We will provide your data export within 30 days.

5. Right to Object (Article 21)

What it means: You can object to processing of your data (especially marketing).

How to request: Click "Unsubscribe" in marketing emails or email us directly.

Response time: We will stop processing within 10 days.

6. Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)

What it means: You can request we limit how we use your data.

How to request: Email privacy@houseinmeta.com with "Restrict Processing Request" in the subject.

‚úì No Discrimination: We will not refuse service or discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

11. Children's Privacy

Dream Home 3D is not intended for children under 16 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16.

If you believe we have collected data from a child under 16, please contact us immediately at privacy@houseinmeta.com, and we will delete the information.

In the EU, children under 16 cannot provide valid consent for data processing. Parents or guardians must provide consent on their behalf.
